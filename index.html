Vision of the Library
Our vision is that of combining human, information resources and facilities that can nurture a university of the highest international standard, whose all-round standing shall be ranked among the best in Nigeria and the world at large.

Our Mission
The mission of PLASU Library is to support the current and anticipated teaching, learning, and research as well as community service activities of the University through provision of relevant information resources, facilities, services and personnel.

Offline Databases
These are archives of electronic journal, e-books, video, audio and/or pictures available on the Intranet.
e-Books – http://10.1.0.14:8080
This is an offline library accessible on the Intranet that store content digitally and is accessible anywhere within the University with computers. Currently, we have 4,200 e-books in the database.

Institutional Repository – http://rep.fulafia.edu.ng:8080/jspui/
Federal University Lafia’s Institutional Repository was established in 2013 as a service for storing and providing access to the outputs (research, workshop, etc.) of Federal University Lafia’s activity.

Learning Management System (LMS) – http://lms.fulafia.edu.ng/
LMS is an online platform where students access their lecture notes and interact with their lecturers. It is design for Administration, Documentation, Tracking, Reporting and Delivery of e-learning education courses or training programmes. The LMS has being effectively utilized by students.

Teeal – http://10.1.0.13
TEEAL (The Essential Electronic Agricultural Library) is a full-text and searchable database of articles from more than 425 high-quality research journals in agriculture and the related sciences.It is a searchable, offline, digital library which contains mainly agriculturally focused reference journals updated annually and delivered by the TEEAL Project Office at Cornell University’s Mann Library.

eGranary Digital Library (Accessed through the eGranary Browser)
The eGranary Digital Library is a collection of documents meant to deliver educational Web content through the Institution Local Area Network (LAN). This way, the documents are available instantly to everyone in the Institution. It provides instant access to over 30 million internet resources including e-books, e-journals, complete and partial websites, software, presentations, videos and audios, etc. to users.

The University Library
Established in 2005, the University Library of the Plateau State University is located at the main campus of the University. The Library has a total holding of over 16,000 volumes of books and 283 current periodical titles. The University library has always been at the centre of research and scholarship, playing a major role in the acquisition, processing and the dissemination of library information materials while responding to patrons’ inquires. We provide outstanding internet connectivity and proficient online access to our e­resources and digital contents through information and communication technology based facilities. Our Clientele have a variety of access routes to conduct efficient research and retrieve relevant information resources. It contains a collection of printed materials, and other e­resources such as e­journals, e­books, electronic theses & dissertations (ETD), technical reports of research organizations, and bibliographic databases.
